ErgoVision Shield Architecture

  • Hydrophobic Coating: Ultra-thin water-repellent coating.
  • Outer Polycarbonate: Main impact-resistant visor.
  • Air Gap: Thermal insulation layer.
  • Inner Polycarbonate: Secondary visor layer.
  • Anti-Fog Coating: Prevents condensation.
  • Silicone Edge Seal: White silicone gasket.
